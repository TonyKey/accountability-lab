# Decision 0001 — One Lab, four research instruments

**Date:** 11 July 2026  
**Status:** Accepted

## Decision

Accountability Lab will be a standalone public research website. Accountability Studio is its interactive centre and contains four independently addressable instruments: Approval Pattern Gallery, Decision Record Explorer, Oversight Console and Accountability Eval Kit.

## Why

One shared site creates a coherent argument and identity while individual routes remain linkable from articles, social posts and outreach. A shared synthetic decision model lets visitors follow accountability from proposed action through intervention, record, supervision and evaluation.

## Boundaries

- No Coupa content, data, terminology or implied affiliation.
- No dependency on or link to WFK.digital for the initial public identity.
- No claim that an interface pattern or evaluation score establishes legal compliance.
- No authentication, persistence or production AI integration in the research foundation.

## Unresolved

- Domain purchase and final production URL.
- Whether public feedback or downloadable assessment reports should be added after observing initial use.
- Which research instrument produces the strongest engagement and expert conversations.
